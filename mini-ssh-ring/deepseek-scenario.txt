## **4. Zoning/Connectivity**

### **Array Ports vs Host WWPNs**
Each array has multiple FC ports (e.g., 0:3:1, 1:3:1). Hosts are zoned to specific ports.

Example mapping for array **s4634**:
- Port 0:3:1 → hosts: host-lnx-222, host-lnx-114, host-esx-131, ...
- Port 1:3:1 → hosts: host-lnx-132, host-lnx-174, host-lnx-289, ...

The switch core-01 connects to s4634's ports and hosts.

### **Host Switch Connectivity**
Each host's FC HBAs connect to specific switch ports. For example:
- host-lnx-222: WWPN 1000...0001 connects to sw-core-01 port 16; WWPN 1000...0002 connects to sw-core-01 port 17 (or to another switch for redundancy).

- s4634, s4378, s4380, s1046 support online upgrade.
